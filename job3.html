Operations Specialist - GTA, File #14622

The second largest burger chain in Canada, A&W Food Services of Canada Inc. is a privately-owned Canadian company that generates over $1 billion in system sales through more than 870 franchised restaurants across the country. A&W has articulated Climate Goals that have created and sustained an exceptional corporate culture. With a steadfast commitment to Climate Goals and strategy, A&W has consistently earned recognition as a highly successful enterprise and one of Canada’s Best Managed Companies.

The Operations Specialist has a mandate to ensure that A&W restaurants are operated to industry-leading standards and that the delivery of the guest experience is a competitive strength for the A&W brand. A key responsibility for the Operations Specialist is to support rapid and profitable expansion in Ontario by effectively coordinating new restaurant openings and by supporting franchisees and the Operations team in Ontario. The Operations Specialist reports to the Regional Business Manager in Ontario and works closely with Business Managers, franchisees, and restaurant managers to foster and sustain restaurant success. A coach and advisor, the Operations Specialist partners with A&W restaurant operators to help them improve their management skills so that A&W restaurants achieve and sustain operational excellence and superior results for sales growth, profitability, and guest satisfaction. The Operations Specialist also works with representatives of A&W’s corporate Marketing, Real Estate and Franchising teams. Based in a home office in the GTA/Golden Horseshoe area, this dynamic career-track role involves up to 40% overnight travel in Ontario as well as in other parts of Eastern Canada, as required.

The ideal candidate has a strong results orientation, an ability to influence others and an aptitude for building exceptional relationships. The candidate must possess a passion for business, problem-solving, operations and customer service. The candidate must have high integrity and excellent analytical, interpersonal, time management and communication skills. With the capacity to multi-task with ease and accuracy, the ideal candidate has sound judgment and the ability to work independently with minimal supervision. Preferably, the candidate has had at least 2 years of operations experience in the hospitality or retail sectors and has been involved in restaurant or new store openings. The candidate must have completed a post-secondary degree in Commerce or Business, a valid driver’s license and the ability and willingness to travel. The ideal candidate is looking for a challenging role and the chance to build a long-term career with an exceptional company. With a long-term view to success, the ideal candidate is a curious risk taker who is open to relocating to other parts of Canada to take on more senior management roles when such opportunities arise.

All qualified candidates are encouraged to reply, in confidence, quoting File #14622, to search@janetdavid.com. Please forward a cover letter and resume as one WORD file. If you require accommodation at any stage during the hiring process, please inform us in advance to arrange reasonable and appropriate accommodation. Information received relating to accommodation measures will be addressed confidentially. We thank all and acknowledge all applicants and will proactively contact those selected for interviews.
