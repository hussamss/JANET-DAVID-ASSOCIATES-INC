Equipment Services Representative, File #14624

The second largest burger chain in Canada, A&W Food Services of Canada Inc. is a privately-owned Canadian company that generates over $1 billion in system sales through more than 870 franchised restaurants across the country. A&W has articulated Climate Goals that have created and sustained an exceptional corporate culture. With a steadfast commitment to Climate Goals and strategy, A&W has consistently earned recognition as a highly successful enterprise and one of Canada’s Best Managed Companies.

The Equipment Services Representative (ESR), supports all A&W restaurants, new and existing, by providing estimates and procurement, and expediting and problem solving services on equipment, décor, and signage orders. Working within the team of Equipment Services Representatives and reporting to the Equipment Services Supervisor, the ESR must develop and maintain superb working relationships with restaurant operators, suppliers, and a wide range of internal departments, including Purchasing & Distribution, Design & Construction, Operations, Marketing, and Accounting.

The candidate must be a team player with sound judgment, a strong drive for results and a commitment to customer service. A flexible and effective multi-tasker who listens to understand, the candidate must maintain a calm and approachable manner in a busy, fast-paced environment and be resourceful in solving problems. The candidate must possess excellent organizational, writing, interpersonal, influencing, and computer skills, and have the ability to work independently. The ideal candidate has completed an undergraduate degree in Commerce or Business Administration and has had at least 2 years of office experience in a dynamic setting. Fluency in English is a requirement for this role. It would be an asset to be fluently bilingual in French and English.

All qualified candidates are encouraged to reply, in confidence, quoting file #14624 to search@janetdavid.com. We thank and acknowledge all candidates and will proactively contact those selected for interviews.
