Client Solutions Business Analyst, File #14611

Tetrad is a small and growing SaaS software services company that incorporates location analytics and GIS mapping to help clients make better business decisions. Tetrad designs highly customizable and flexible Sitewise solutions for organizations in the restaurant, retail, real estate, healthcare and financial services sectors across North America. Curious and proactive, the team at Tetrad works closely with clients to deeply understand their needs so that the team can deliver the right mix of data, tools and support services that clients need to solve complex business challenges.

To support strategic growth, Tetrad wishes to add a Client Solutions Business Analyst (CSBA) to the existing core team comprised of three CSBA’s and one Team Leader. Working with Tetrad’s Sitewise mapping and location analytics platform, each CSBA implements and maintains customized solutions to enable end-users to make faster and more efficient site selection decisions. The role interfaces with existing and potential clients as well as the Tetrad sales and management teams.

Key responsibilities include:

  • Building out new Sitewise GIS systems and performing pre-launch quality assurance testing;
  • Translating client needs and requirements into written Sitewise solution implementation plans;
  • Serving clients, post launch, by providing on-going product support, training and troubleshooting;
  • Communicating with the internal sales and management teams to understand existing and prospective customers'business processes, analytical and reporting requirements; and
  • Providing status updates to the sales and management teams.

The successful candidate must be a curious, creative thinker with leadership potential and a passion for business, technology and complex problem solving. With abundant common sense and the ability to learn quickly and to deal positively with obstacles, the candidate must have sound judgment and strong logic and quantitative skills. The candidate must have solid business acumen and a high degree of self-awareness. With superb analytical, communications, presentation and interpersonal skills, the candidate must be able to work both independently and as a team player in a fast-paced context with competing priorities and multiple projects. It would be an asset to be a power user with experience in FME/Alteryx and/or MapInfo/ArcGIS. The candidate must have at least a Bachelor’s degree in a related discipline such as business, commerce, engineering, technology or GIS. Ideally, the candidate will have some previous experience with Geographic Information Systems (GIS) development. Preferably, the candidate will have at least 2 years of relevant work experience and proven supervisory expertise. Experience in, and exposure to, retail analytics and predictive modeling would be helpful.

Located in contemporary, open-concept offices on 5th Avenue, near Burrard Street, in the heart of Vancouver, Tetrad’s culture is customer-centric, nimble, transparent and collaborative. Casual attire is the office norm. Tetrad offers competitive compensation and benefits, including base salary, profit sharing, matching RRSP, extended health and dental plans and a Health and Wellness plan. To learn more about Tetrad, please go to www.Tetrad.com
