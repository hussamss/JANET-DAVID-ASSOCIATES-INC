Controller, File #14623

The second largest burger chain in Canada, A&W Food Services of Canada Inc. is a privately-owned Canadian company that generates over $1 billion in system sales through more than 870 franchised restaurants across the country. A&W has articulated Climate Goals that have created and sustained an exceptional corporate culture. With a steadfast commitment to Climate Goals and strategy, A&W has consistently earned recognition as a highly successful enterprise and one of Canada’s Best Managed Companies.

Based at A&W’s Corporate Home Office in North Vancouver, the Controller is responsible for all financial reporting for the Company and related private entities as well as for the publicly traded Income Fund. The Controller plays a pivotal role in corporate planning and budgeting, corporate tax activities, in safeguarding fixed assets and working capital and in providing proactive interpretation of financial data by the Company.

The Controller is one of 5 direct reports to the Chief Financial Officer. The Accounting Administrator, Intermediate Accountant, Payroll Administrator and Benefits Administrator report to the Controller. The Controller interfaces with a wide range of internal and external stakeholders including senior executives, managers, Trustees, lawyers, auditors, bankers and representatives of government agencies, including tax authorities.

The candidate must be a Chartered Professional Accountant with at least 7 years of progressive financial management experience. The ideal candidate has strong business acumen and a passion for accounting, financial reporting, problem solving and informed decision-making. The candidate must have a superb eye for detail and be willing and able to be hands-on without losing sight of the big picture. The candidate must possess sound knowledge of financial controls, full-cycle accounting, tax, payroll and benefits administration as well as proven expertise in financial and corporate reporting, and risk management. A strategic thinker who can anticipate and navigate change, the candidate must have the proven ability to successfully balance multiple priorities and achieve accurate results on time and with relationships intact. A proactive person with leadership attributes, high integrity, excellent analytical, writing, and oral communication skills, the candidate must also have strong organizational and time management skills.

All qualified candidates are encouraged to reply, in confidence, quoting file #14623, to search@janetdavid.com. We thank and acknowledge all candidates and will proactively contact those selected for interviews.
