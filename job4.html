Front-End Software Engineer, File #14615

Tetrad is a small and growing SaaS software and services company that incorporates GIS mapping and location analytics to help clients make better business decisions. Tetrad designs highly customizable and flexible solutions for organizations in the restaurant, retail, real estate, healthcare and financial services sectors across North America. Curious and proactive, the team at Tetrad takes time to get to know clients to deeply understand their needs so that the Tetrad team can deliver the right mix of data, analytical tools and support services that clients need to solve complex business challenges.

Tetrad is moving from a native to hybrid approach in the development of applications for desktop, tablets and mobile phone environment. Successful growth has created an opportunity for an experienced software engineer to join the small, nimble core technical team in the creation of enterprise-class software products. The role will report to the Chief Technology Officer. While the emphasis of the Front-End Software Engineer role will be on developing front-end applications, the role will also has a mandate to understand and support back-end systems development.

  • Designing, coding and debugging web and mobile applications in various software languages;
  • Designing, evaluating and implementing web user interfaces;
  • Interfacing with mapping APIs;
  • Designing and writing clear, well documented, efficient code;
  • Software testing and quality assurance; and
  • Support, maintain and document software functionality

To meet deadlines and to provide exceptional customer service across different time zones, the Front-End Software Engineer will require working “off hours”, from time to time.

The ideal candidate for the role of Front-End Software Engineer is a curious, proactive problem solver who learns quickly and who anticipates issues. With a Bachelor’s degree in computer science or related field, the candidate must have 3 to 4 years of experience in building web and mobile applications using Angular and jQuery as well as experience in using PhoneGap, Bing Maps and/or Google Maps. It would be an asset to have a strong understanding of mapping and GIS technology. While the primary accountability for the role will be on developing front-end applications, the candidate must have an authentic interest in, and aptitude for, both developing software for both the front-end and back-end.

An agile and creative thinker with the ability to identify and evaluate appropriate tools and technologies to use in software development, in the context of a rapidly changing open-source environment, the candidate must have sound knowledge of database architecture, JavaScript, C-Sharp, HTML and the .NET framework. The candidate must have a proven aptitude for adapting new technologies and tools in the design, testing and implementation of user-friendly applications for desktop, tablet and mobile phone users. The candidate must have excellent communication skills, both written and oral, an aptitude for business and the ability to interpret written requirements and technical specification documents. The candidate must have abundant common sense and a strong drive to achieve results, on time and on budget. Flexible and calm under pressure, the ideal candidatesidate is a self-starter and team player who can work with minimal supervision.

Located in contemporary, open-concept offices on 5th Avenue near Burrard in the heart of Vancouver, Tetrad's culture is customer-centric, nimble, transparent and collaborative. Casual attire is the office norm. Tetrad offers competitive compensatin and benefits, including base salary, profit sharing, matching RRSP, extended health and dental plans and a Health and Wellness plan. To learn more about Tetrad, please go to www.Tetrad.com
